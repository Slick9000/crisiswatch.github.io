Disaster Preparedness Tips

Home Preparation

  • Secure heavy furniture to walls
  • Know how to turn off utilities
  • Install smoke and CO detectors
  • Consider earthquake-proofing

Emergency Kit

  • 3-day supply of water (1 gal/person/day)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First aid kit
  • Flashlight + batteries

Communication Plan

  • Establish meeting places
  • Designate out-of-area contact
  • Keep important numbers written down
  • Know emergency alert systems

Food & Essential Resources

Emergency Food Supplies

  • Non-perishable foods: Canned goods, dried fruits, nuts, granola bars
  • Comfort foods: Cookies, hard candy, instant coffee
  • Special needs: Baby food, pet food, dietary restrictions
  • Preparation: Manual can opener, disposable utensils

Water Storage

  • Store at least 1 gallon per person per day (3-day minimum)
  • Replace stored water every 6 months
  • Have water purification tablets or filters

Prepare Your Kit

Plan Your Route

Have multiple evacuation routes planned.

Practice Drills

Regularly practice evacuation with family.

Evacuation Checklist

  • Emergency kit and supplies
  • Important documents
  • Medications
  • Cell phone & charger
  • Cash and cards
  • Clothing & pet supplies

Earthquake Safety

Before: Secure furniture, know safe spots.

During: Drop, cover, and hold on.

After: Check for injuries and listen for updates.
