Mission of CrisisWatch

We develop an informative and resourceful solution to aid Caribbean countries such as Barbados, Jamaica and Trinidad in disaster preparedness. Our hope for this is that more citizens in those countries will be better educated about the shelters,resources and life saving tips that can save their life.
